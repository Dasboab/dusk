Operation First Light

Commander, the sun never fully rises here. A hostile stronghold holds the far ridge and the eastern sea lane is unguarded. Establish power, extract auren ore, field a modern combined force, take the coast from a Naval Yard, and level their Construction Yard. Veterans grow stronger with every kill.

Touch: drag to pan (flick to glide), pinch to zoom, tap to select and order. Hold still then drag for box select, or arm the Box button and drag.
Mouse: left click or drag selects, right click orders, wheel zooms, WASD or edges pan.
Command bar: Attack move, Patrol, Guard, Scout, Board IFVs, TYP grabs every unit of the selected type, Cohorts I to III.
Navy: place the Naval Yard on the eastern shoreline. MLRS strikes far beyond its own sight.
H home, X stop, Esc cancel.
