DUSK

Operation Dawn Convoy · v0.5

Commander, the sun never fully rises here. Your base convoy is rolling in from the west while a hostile stronghold holds the far ridge. Escort the Mobile HQ to open ground, press DEP to deploy it into a Construction Yard, then build power, extract auren ore and field a combined force: armour, drones, counter drone teams and a navy. Level their Construction Yard to win.

Touch: drag to pan (flick to glide), pinch to zoom, tap to select and order. Hold still then drag for box select, or arm the Box button and drag.
Mouse: left click or drag selects, right click orders, wheel zooms, WASD or edges pan.
Command bar: Attack move, Patrol, Guard, Scout, Board IFVs, TYP grabs every unit of the selected type, Cohorts I to III.
Navy: place the Naval Yard on the eastern shoreline. Landing Craft carry 8: park it touching the beach, board with BRD, unload with UNL.
Drones: Drone Bay unlocks Recon and FPV strike drones, Robotics builds Drone Dogs and Drone Tanks, the EW Centre trains Counter UAS teams, the Air Base fields Strike UAVs.
H home, X stop, Esc cancel.
